code/__DEFINES/status_effects.dm

These are all the different status effects. Use the paths for each effect in the defines.

STATUS_EFFECT_MULTIPLEif it allows multiple instances of the effect
STATUS_EFFECT_UNIQUEif it allows only one, preventing new instances
STATUS_EFFECT_REPLACEif it allows only one, but new instances replace
STATUS_EFFECT_REFRESHif it only allows one, and new instances just instead refresh the timer
STATUS_EFFECT_SHADOW_MENDQuick, powerful heal that deals damage afterwards. Heals 15 brute/burn every second for 3 seconds.
STATUS_EFFECT_VANGUARDGrants temporary stun absorption, but will stun the user based on how many stuns they absorbed.
STATUS_EFFECT_INATHNEQS_ENDOWMENTA 15-second invulnerability and stun absorption, granted by Inath-neq.
STATUS_EFFECT_POWERREGENRegenerates power on a given cyborg over time
STATUS_EFFECT_HISGRACEHis Grace.
STATUS_EFFECT_WISH_GRANTERS_GIFTIf you're currently resurrecting with the Wish Granter
STATUS_EFFECT_BLOODDRUNKStun immunity and greatly reduced damage taken
STATUS_EFFECT_FLESHMENDVery fast healing; suppressed by fire, and heals less fire damage
STATUS_EFFECT_EXERCISEDPrevents heart disease
STATUS_EFFECT_HIPPOCRATIC_OATHGives you an aura of healing as well as regrowing the Rod of Asclepius if lost
STATUS_EFFECT_ANTIMAGICgrants antimagic (and reapplies if lost) for the duration
STATUS_EFFECT_STUNthe affected is unable to move or use items
STATUS_EFFECT_KNOCKDOWNthe affected is unable to stand up
STATUS_EFFECT_IMMOBILIZEDthe affected is unable to move
STATUS_EFFECT_PARALYZEDthe affected is unable to move, use items, or stand up.
STATUS_EFFECT_UNCONSCIOUSthe affected is unconscious
STATUS_EFFECT_SLEEPINGthe affected is asleep
STATUS_EFFECT_PACIFYthe affected is pacified, preventing direct hostile actions
STATUS_EFFECT_BELLIGERENTforces the affected to walk, doing damage if they try to run
STATUS_EFFECT_GEISTRACKERif you're using geis, this tracks that and keeps you from using scripture
STATUS_EFFECT_MANIAMOTORdisrupts, damages, and confuses the affected as long as they're in range of the motor
MAX_MANIA_SEVERITYhow high the mania severity can go
MANIA_DAMAGE_TO_CONVERThow much damage is required before it'll convert affected targets
STATUS_EFFECT_CHOKINGSTRANDChoking Strand
STATUS_EFFECT_HISWRATHHis Wrath.
STATUS_EFFECT_SUMMONEDGHOSTis a cult ghost and can't use manifest runes
STATUS_EFFECT_CRUSHERMARKif struck with a proto-kinetic crusher, takes a ton of damage
STATUS_EFFECT_SAWBLEEDif the bleed builds up enough, takes a ton of damage
CURSE_BLINDINGmakes the edges of the target's screen obscured
CURSE_SPAWNINGspawns creatures that attack the target only
CURSE_WASTINGcauses gradual damage
CURSE_GRASPINGhands reach out from the sides of the screen, doing damage and stunning if they hit the target
STATUS_EFFECT_KINDLEA knockdown reduced by 1 second for every 3 points of damage the target takes.
STATUS_EFFECT_ICHORIAL_STAINPrevents a servant from being revived by vitality matrices for one minute.
STATUS_EFFECT_GONBOLAPACIFYGives the user gondola traits while the gonbola is attached to them.
STATUS_EFFECT_SPASMScauses random muscle spasms
STATUS_EFFECT_DNA_MELTusually does something horrible to you when you hit 100 genetic instability
STATUS_EFFECT_GO_AWAYmakes you launch through walls in a single direction for a while
STATUS_EFFECT_STASISHalts biological functions like bleeding, chemical processing, blood regeneration, walking, etc
STATUS_EFFECT_CRUSHERDAMAGETRACKINGtracks total kinetic crusher damage on a target
STATUS_EFFECT_SYPHONMARKtracks kills for the KA death syphon module
STATUS_EFFECT_INLOVEDisplays you as being in love with someone else, and makes hearts appear around them.
STATUS_EFFECT_BUGGEDLets other mobs listen in on what it hears
